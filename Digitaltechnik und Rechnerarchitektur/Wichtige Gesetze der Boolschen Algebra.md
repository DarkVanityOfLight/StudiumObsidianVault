
## Axiome

### Kommutativität
$$
a + b = b + a \\
ab = ba
$$

### Distributivität
$$
a(b + c) = ab + ac \\
a + (bc) = (a + b)(a + c)
$$

### Neutrale Elemente
$$
a + 0 = a \\
a \cdot 1 = a
$$

### Komplement
$$
a + a = 1 \\
a \cdot a = 0
$$

## Wichtige Gesetze

### Idempotenz
$$
a + a = a \\
aa = a
$$

### Maximale Elemente
$$
a + 1 = 1 \\
a \cdot 0 = 0
$$

### Absorption
$$
a + ab = a \\
a \cdot (a + b) = a
$$

### Assoziativität
$$
a + (b + c) = (a + b) + c \\
a(bc) = (ab)c
$$

### deMorgan's Gesetze
$$
(a + b) = ab \\
(ab) = a + b
$$

### Involution
$$
a = a
$$

### Konsens
$$
ab + ac + bc = ab + ac \\
(a + b)(a + c)(b + c) = (a + b)(a + c)
$$
