### a)
$L_p = [Ag^+] \cdot [Cl^-]$
$[Ag+] = [Cl^-]$
$$\begin{align}
&10^{-10} \frac{mol^2}{L^2} = [Ag^+]^2\\
\iff&\sqrt{10^{-10}} \frac{mol}{L} =  [Ag^+]\\
\iff&[Ag^+] = \sqrt{10^{-10}} \frac{mol}{L}
\end{align}$$

### b)

$$Mol(NaCl) = \frac{69.6g}{23\frac{g}{mol} +36.5\frac{g}{mol}} = 1.17mol$$

$$[CL^-] = 1.17 \frac{mol}{L}$$

$$\begin{align}
&L_p = [Ag^+] \cdot [Cl^-]\\
&[Ag^+] = \frac{L_p}{[Cl^-]}\\
&[Ag^{+}] = \frac{10^{-10}\frac{mol^2}{L^2}}{1.17\frac{mol}{L}}
\end{align}$$
