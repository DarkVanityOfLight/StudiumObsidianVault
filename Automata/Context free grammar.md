Let $G = (V, \Sigma, P, S)$ be a context-free grammar where
- $V$ is the finite set of variables or nonterminals
- $\Sigma$ is the finite terminal alphabet
- $P\subseteq V \times (V\cup \Sigma)^*$ is a finite set of productions
- $S\in V$ is the start variable

The [language](Sprache.md) of $G$ is denoted $L(G) \subseteq \Sigma^*$.
