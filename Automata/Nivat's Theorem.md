A class of [languages](Sprache.md) $\mathcal C$ is closed under [Rational Transductions](Rational%20Transductions.md) (i.e., $\mathcal C$ is a full trio) iff it is closed under each of the following operations:
1. Intersection with a [regular language](Reguläre%20Sprachen.md)
2. [Homomorphism](Homomorphism.md)
3. inverse homomorphism
