Let $\Gamma$ be a [subgroup](Gruppe.md#Untergruppenkriterium) of the [semantic symmetries](Semantic%20Symmetry.md) of $F$. 

For two complete [assignments](Assignment.md) $\tau$ and $\tau'$, we write $\tau \sim\tau'$ iff there is a $\varphi \in\Gamma$ such that $\varphi(\tau) = \tau'$. $\tau\sim\tau'$ means they are in the same [orbit](Orbit.md) of $\Gamma$.
