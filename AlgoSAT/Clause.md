A clause $C$ is a disjunction of [Literals](AlgoSAT/Literal.md). It can be falsified with exactly one assignment to its literals, namely assigning all literals to false.
There are $2^k-1$ satisfying assignments to its $k$ literals.

The special empty clause (written $\bot$) is always falsified.
