> An _automorphism_ of a [Graph](AlgoSAT/Graph.md) $G$ is an [Graph Isomorphism](Graph%20Isomorphism.md) of $G$ onto itself

Usually, we are interested in all automorphisms of a graph.

The _automorphism group_ $Aut(G)$ of a graph $G$ is the [set](Mengen.md) that contains all automorphisms of $G$. This set forms a _permutation_ [group](Gruppe.md).

A permutation is a [bijection](Bijektiv.md) $\varphi: V\to V$.
