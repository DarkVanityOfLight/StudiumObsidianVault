For an [assignment](Assignment.md) $\alpha$ to a formula $F$ we may also write $\alpha\vDash F$, to denote that $\alpha$ satisfies $F$.

For formulas (or sets of formulas) $F$ and $G$, we write 
$$F \vDash G$$
to mean that every assignment satisfying $F$ also satisfies $G$, or formally
$$\forall \alpha: \alpha\vDash F \implies \alpha\vDash G$$
In other words $F$ _entails_ $G$.
