A connected undirected graph $S$ is an _unavoidable subgraph_ in the Graph $G$ if every red/blue edge coloring of the edges of $G$ contains $S$ either in red or in blue.

The Ramsey number $R(r, s)$, is defined as the smallest $n$ such that all red/blue edge-colorings of $K_n$ have a red $r$ clique or a blue $s$ clique.
