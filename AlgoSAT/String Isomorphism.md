Let $\Omega$ be a finite [set](Mengen.md) and $\Sigma$ a finite [Alphabet](Alphabet.md), and $\Gamma \le Sym(\Omega)$ a permutation [group](Gruppe.md).

A _string_ is a function $\tau :\Omega \to \Sigma$. For $\varphi\in\Gamma$, define the action
$$(\varphi(\tau))(v) := \tau(\varphi(v))$$

Two strings $\tau_1, \tau_2\in\Sigma^\Omega$  are $\Gamma$ [isomorphic](Graph%20Isomorphism.md), written $\tau_1 \cong \tau_2$, if there exists $\varphi\in \Gamma$ such that
$$\varphi(\tau_1) = \tau_2$$
Or equivalently, $\tau_1$ and $\tau_2$ lie in the same [Orbit](Orbit.md) under $\Gamma$.
