$B \geq 2$ $\phi_{B,r}: \{0, ..., B-1\}^r \to {0, ..., B^r-1}$
$(a_{r-1}, ..., a_1, a_0) \mapsto \sum^{r-1}_{i=0}a_i B^i$
$\phi_{10, 3}((0, 2, 3)) = 0\cdot 10^2 +2 \cdot 10^1 + 3\cdot 10^0 = 23$
